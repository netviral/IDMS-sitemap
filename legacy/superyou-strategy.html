Slide 1 — Website Goal

Website Goal

Primary Goal
Convert first-time visitors into repeat buyers of a novel protein format they've never heard of before.
The core tension

SuperYou sells fermented yeast protein — a completely unfamiliar ingredient. The site must do two jobs simultaneously: educate fast and sell confidently.

The single sentence goal
"Help a skeptical, health-conscious Indian consumer understand why fermented yeast protein is better for their gut — and buy their first pack in under 3 minutes."
Business goals (from sitemap signals)
  • Drive product sales across wafers, powders, and creatine
  • Build a subscription/loyalty base (SuperYou Pro, Refer & Earn)
  • Establish authority in the fermented protein niche (20+ blog posts)
  • Expand into merchandise (shakers, pro shaker — community building)
What the site is NOT doing well yet
  • No quiz, no bundle builder, no "start here" flow for new visitors
  • Pro/affiliate program is buried in /pages — not part of a growth funnel
  • No visible blog category structure beyond one umbrella topic

Slide 3 — Consumer Flow

3 distinct visitor types

Type A
Curious Newcomer

Landed from an ad. Skeptical. Has never heard of fermented yeast protein.

Ad → HP → Education → PDP → Checkout

Gap: No clear bridge from homepage to education.

Type B
Comparison Shopper

Already uses protein. Searching for an upgrade via Google.

Google → Blog → Collections → PDP → Checkout

Status: Well-served by strong Blog SEO cluster.

Type C
Repeat Buyer

Knows the brand, coming back for a specific product or bundle.

Direct URL → Collections → PDP/Bundle → Checkout

Gap: No reorder flow or subscription.

Critical drop-off points to fix
  • Homepage → no clear next step for first-timers
  • Blog posts → no inline add-to-cart or product embed
  • Collections/all-products are duplicate dead ends
  • SuperYou Pro buried — needs post-purchase flow

Slide 4 — Content Engine

20-post cluster. Disconnected engine.

Topical Authority: FYP

The 20-post blog targets fermented yeast protein. Smart niche play with low competition.

  • Comparison — vs Whey, vs Soy (capturing switchers)
  • Gut & Digestion — bloating, IBS (high conversion)
  • Performance — muscle, endurance (aspiration)
  • Science — amino acids, fermentation process (trust)
  • Brand story — Behind the scenes (credibility)
    • What's missing
    • Video content — zero video URLs in sitemap
    • UGC layer — no /reviews or community path
    • Comparison page — no dedicated landing page
    • Recipes — no "how to use daily" content
    • Regional — India-specific deficiency stats
    Recommended schedule: 2 blogs/month sustaining cluster + 1 "vs" post targeting competitor keywords. Huge win: Add product CTAs inside existing posts.

    Slide 6 — Interaction model

    From Browse-and-buy to Engage-and-build

    1. Ingredient Explainer

    Scroll-driven breakdown of the fermentation process. Make the unfamiliar feel premium.

    2. "Find Your Protein" Quiz

    Preference → Goal → Lifestyle quiz with product + blog output.

    3. Blog → Product Bridge

    Inline product cards inside blog posts to bridge education and commerce.

    4. Bundle Customiser

    "Build your stack" logic surfaced visually: protein + creatine + shaker.

    5. Subscription Toggle

    One-time vs Subscribe & Save to increase LTV dramatically.

    6. Gut Health Tracker

    SuperYou Pro experience to track feelings week-by-week. UGC generator.
